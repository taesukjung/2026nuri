HR System Based on Job Role and Performance Evaluation

We motivate our employees to enhance their expertise through a compensation and evaluation system that considers job value and roles. By providing fair performance evaluations and training opportunities for professional growth, we are laying the foundation for the global talent management that Nuri Infos strives for. We are building a corporate culture that values young, talented individuals, offering opportunities to those who demonstrate performance and competence, moving away from a seniority-based system
