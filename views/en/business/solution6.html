Nuri Infos Anyworks SFA is an integrated field sales management solution that manages the field sales activities of sales representatives/promoters in charge of sales/promotion for companies.

We provide a total solution for managing various work types and field tasks of employees managed by your company. You can handle all tasks related to field workers and personnel management at once, and it provides functions for managing field workers, such as HR, promotion, sales, and payroll, as well as quickly providing field conditions and various statistics within the organization.

Service
01Business Site Task Management
Through connection with the sales system, such as reporting various statuses of the business site, events, and sales target management, it is possible to efficiently process tasks in the field and quickly share information with the head office.
02Employee Management
Processes various HR data of field workers (work plan, arrival/departure, overtime work, leave processing) in the field, and simplifies the work procedures of field and head office managers by linking with HR/payroll systems, such as field worker evaluation and payroll.
03Task Reporting
Shares various task report data generated in the field with the head office in real time, and links the processing of shared information with the field, reducing the lead time of information communication and enabling immediate sales activities.
04Web Standard UI Provided
Provides a management web screen that complies with web standards and considers user convenience, and by providing a mobile app (Android, iOS), it is possible to share the same information view in the field and at the head office.

Benefits

  • Field Worker Convenience

    Field workers can immediately process visit (work) plans, GPS-based arrival/departure management, field survey tasks (display, fixtures, events, store openings, competitors), and target progress management on mobile in the field.

  • Convenience by User Level

    Sales managers become more comfortable - It is possible to check the field situation of field workers in real time, and by reducing the lead time for information confirmation, immediate field work instructions
