Anyworks BIS is a broadcasting solution optimized for various business systems of broadcasting companies.

Service
01Application Scope
Broadcasting tasks, including programming, production, broadcasting/operation, advertising, promotion, content, art supplies, production cost billing, broadcasting equipment management, and trust deed management.
02Various Advantages
Not dependent on a specific hardware OS (JAVA-based). A user-centered system to improve work efficiency. System construction that guarantees scalability and stability. Provides various reporting functions.
03Integrated System
Provides APIs for integration with internal systems such as ERP and groupware. Through this, it is possible to implement linkage with legacy systems such as billing settlement, vouchers, and sales information.

Benefits

  • Stable Solution

    A stable solution based on a proven, up-to-date framework. All functions are modular, making it a proven solution with excellent reusability and scalability.

  • Customized Solution

    Based on the technology and know-how accumulated over 20 years as a performer of various public broadcasting company projects, we support the construction of an optimal broadcasting system.

  • External Integration-Oriented Structure

    Supports integration by establishing an interface system with TV broadcasting systems, NPS, radio integration systems, media integration systems, and KOBACO.
