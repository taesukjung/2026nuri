A systematic supply chain relationship management solution!

SRM (Supplier Relationship Management) efficiently manages the entire process from supplier evaluation/selection to contract, delivery, and evaluation, contributing to business performance improvement by improving transparency and efficiency in the supply chain.

Service
01 Supplier Evaluation/Selection
· Supports online/offline supplier evaluation and selection, and contract progress.
· Enhances work efficiency by enabling online registration of various application forms required for supplier contracts.
· Simplifies the complicated supplier selection process through electronic contracts.
02Cultural Facility
· Provides interworking service in connection with cultural facilities.
· Supports ticket issuance and reservation integration in conjunction with the cultural facility-linked system.
· Provides convenient facility usage service.
03Franchise/Direct Store POS System
· Improves sales by operating a stable system, enhancing customer reliability.
· Supports the activation of affiliate sales by providing a store-customized POS.
· Operates integrated stores nationwide/Provides real-time sales and inventory management.

Benefits

  • Cost Reduction

    Reduces order loss rate by placing orders based on available inventory, and prevents excessive inventory through accurate inventory management, resulting in reduced warehouse maintenance costs.
    It is also possible to expect a reduction in inventory maintenance costs by reducing the number of management personnel.

  • Increased Partner Management Efficiency

    Shift from an organization-centric to a market-centric and customer-centric paradigm. Increased online communication leads to greater partner satisfaction with services.
    It also ensures transaction transparency, maintains compliance, and strengthens governance.

  • Increased Sales Productivity

    Real-time business processing between headquarters and partners (suppliers, in this case) is possible, leading to an increase in sales productivity.
    Timely and adequate supply is possible in terms of product supply, and the incidence of bad debts is reduced through real-time distribution point analysis.
