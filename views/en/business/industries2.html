Finance

Driving the necessary evolution of financial institutions in the digital finance era through Digital Transformation.

The financial industry is facing a period of significant change, driven by socioeconomic shifts like low growth, an aging population, and globalization, as well as advancements in digital technologies such as mobile, big data, cloud computing, and social networks. These changes necessitate business model innovation. In this environment, Digital Transformation is a key element for survival. Nuri Infos provides superior services to implement this transformation, drawing upon extensive experience in various financial sectors, including banking, securities, and brokerage.
